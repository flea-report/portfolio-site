## Approach

The foundation of the project has been understanding the needs of small businesses and designing an experience that makes ecommerce accessible — while remaining flexible enough to support a wide range of business types and product offerings.

Working in a fast-moving asynchronous startup means being adaptable about when and how design plays a role. Depending on capacity, features can be designed before development, or built first and refined later.

## Outcomes

As of May 2026 Sendd has grown to 129 stores and has expanded beyond the NZ market.

## Key learnings

Working in a startup environment is fairly new to me and it has required a different mindset in terms of understanding my role as a designer. Scope and prioritisation can shift rapidly and being able to pivot and make adjustments have been valuable learnings.

Understanding how AI can play a role as part of a feature's life cycle has also been illuminating.
